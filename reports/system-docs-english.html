1. Executive Summary

Marbella Golf Times is a premium golf tee-time booking platform specifically designed for the Costa del Sol region in Spain. The platform serves as a personal concierge service, aggregating tee times from over 40 golf courses and providing golfers with a seamless experience to find, compare, and book their perfect round.

üéØ Platform Purpose

The platform bridges the gap between golfers seeking tee times and the premier golf courses of southern Spain. By aggregating availability from multiple booking providers (Golfmanager, TeeOne) and direct course partnerships, we offer comprehensive coverage of the Costa del Sol golf scene.

üë• Target Users

Visiting Golfers

Tourists and holiday-makers seeking tee times at Costa del Sol's finest courses. They value easy discovery, transparent pricing, and reliable booking.

Local Residents

Expats and locals who regularly play golf and want to explore different courses with the convenience of a single platform.

Golf Groups

Organised golf societies and groups planning multi-round trips who need to coordinate bookings across several courses.

Course Administrators

Golf course staff who manage their course listings, respond to booking requests, and track commissions.

‚ú® Key Features

  • Course Discovery: Browse 47 courses with photos, descriptions, and real-time availability
  • Smart Search: Filter by date, time, players, price range, and location
  • Weather Integration: 5-day forecast for each course location
  • Multi-Language: Full support for English, Spanish, Danish, Swedish, and Russian
  • Mobile Optimised: Responsive design works perfectly on all devices
  • Admin Dashboard: Complete management interface for bookings, courses, and communications
  • Email System: Integrated inbox for course partnership communications
  • Commission Tracking: Monitor affiliate earnings from each course partnership
